Simple Present Tense

Use it for:

  • Habits and routines (I drink coffee every morning)
  • Facts and general truths (The sun rises in the east)
  • Permanent situations (She lives in London)
  • Scheduled events (The train leaves at 6 PM)
Examples:
✓ I work at a bank.
✓ She plays tennis every weekend.
✓ They don't like pizza.
✓ Does he speak Spanish?

Present Continuous Tense

Use it for:

  • Actions happening now (I am reading a book)
  • Temporary situations (He is staying with friends this week)
  • Current trends (More people are using electric cars)
  • Future arrangements (We are meeting tomorrow at 5)
Examples:
✓ I am studying English right now.
✓ She is watching TV at the moment.
✓ They aren't working today.
✓ Are you coming to the party?
💡 Quick Tip: Look for signal words!
Simple Present: always, usually, often, sometimes, never, every day/week
Present Continuous: now, at the moment, currently, today, this week, right now
